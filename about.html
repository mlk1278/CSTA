The CSTA NOVA Mission

CSTA NOVA aspires to integrate teachers and students within the Northern Virginia community and provide them a diversified experience in computer science.

HOW WILL WE ACHIEVE OUR MISSION?

There are multiple reasons why CSTA NOVA membership is significant.
CROSS-FUNCTIONAL LEADERSHIP TEAM

CSTA has constructed a holistic leadership structure to provide computer science and IT education for public schools and increase the commitment of teachers towards event organization.

IDENTIFYING KEY GROUPS IN THE COMPUTER SCIENCE COMMUNITY

Since many students wish to pursue distinct majors, by demonstrating that fields like biology and physics still implement CS, we can vary the students and teachers that participate in CSTA NOVA.

UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITIONS

CSTA NOVA desires to establish a purpose for each future member in its organization as students, teachers, industry leaders, and corporations will contribute differently to the organization making them equally significant.

RECRUITMENT AND EVENT PLANNING

CSTA NOVA will organize internships, conferences, and identify computer science companies to collaborate with so that students can obtain essential experience and industry connections.
