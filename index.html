What sets the CSTA NOVA chapter apart from other organizations?

WHO WE ARE

There are many groups that attempt to expand the rapidly emerging subject of computer science in academic and extracurricular environments through the Northern Virginia area. However, there is only one group that takes a multi-functional approach to unify the computer science community and that is CSTA NOVA.

Contact Now

WHAT WE OFFER

There are various services and programs that CSTA NOVA offers.

EDUCATIONAL INTEGRATION

CSTA NOVA aspires to immerse computer science curriculum in K8 classes and expose students to predominant applications of the field such as IoT, AI, and web/mobile develompent.

ONLINE ENVIRONMENT

By developing a cloud drive, CSTA NOVA has facilitated teachers to share computer science labs, projects, programming assignments, and ideas that not only engage students but increase the diversification of CS curriculum.

COMMUNICATING OPPORTUNITIES

The CSTA NOVA chapter has ensured that students will be informed of upcoming computer science events such as Hackathons, symposiums, and competitions through an interactive calendar, social media, and other outreach methods.

Why does the CSTA NOVA chapter successfully promote the computer science community?

WHY REGISTER WITH US

There are multiple reasons why CSTA NOVA membership is significant.
ACCESS TO OPTIMAL COMPUTER SCIENCE EDUCATION

Through the CSTA Northern Virginia chapter, teachers can disperse their CS curriculum to make students and teachers identify unique ways to stimulate CS education.

A GROWING DEMAND FOR COMPUTATIONAL THINKERS

Because major technologies such as AI, virtual reality, IoT, and bioinformatics have emerged, the demand for computer science jobs has propelled organizations like CSTA to promote the field.

VERSATILE OPPORTUNITIES AND RESOURCES

CSTA NOVA has retained a motivation to offer internships, research programs, career training, and conferences that will cultivate expertise within students for future computer science careers.
